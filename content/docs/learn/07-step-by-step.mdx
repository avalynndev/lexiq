# Step-by-Step Instructions

Breaking down tasks into steps helps the AI follow your instructions more accurately. Step-by-step prompts are especially useful for complex or multi-stage tasks, ensuring that the AI doesn't skip important details and follows a logical order.

## Why Use Step-by-Step Prompts?

- **Improves accuracy:** The AI is less likely to miss steps or make mistakes.
- **Enhances clarity:** Each step is clearly defined, reducing ambiguity.
- **Supports learning:** Step-by-step instructions are great for educational content or tutorials.

<Steps>
  <Step>
    Ask the AI to list the steps before completing the task. This helps the
    model plan its response.
  </Step>
  <Step>
    Use numbered or bulleted lists in your prompt to make the structure clear.
  </Step>
  <Step>
    Be explicit about the order of operations and any dependencies between
    steps.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Example

```
List the steps to bake a chocolate cake.
```

**Expanded Example:**

```
List the steps to bake a chocolate cake, including preparation, baking, and cooling. For each step, provide a brief explanation of why it is important.
```

## Best Practices for Step-by-Step Prompts

- Break down complex tasks into manageable steps.
- Ask the AI to explain each step if you want more detail.
- Use step-by-step prompts for troubleshooting, instructions, or multi-part questions.
